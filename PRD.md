# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Project Overview
Gematria Guru is a website designed to perform gematria calculations.

## Goals
- Provide an easy-to-use platform for users interested in gematria.
- Ensure fast and accurate computation of gematria values.
- Deliver a seamless user experience with an intuitive interface.

## Key Features
- Perform gematria calculations for a variety of texts.
- Support different gematria systems and configurations.
- Display results clearly and interactively.

## Target Audience
- Individuals interested in numerology and gematria.
- Researchers or enthusiasts in the field of mysticism and numerical analysis.

## Success Metrics
- User acquisition and retention rates.
- Performance and accuracy in text calculations.
- Positive user feedback on usability.
